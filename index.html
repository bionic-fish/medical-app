About The Project


A personal project dedicated to helping people diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes monitor their blood glucose levels through a smartphone or smart watch application.



Through research and persistance I believe I am able to create an app that can pair with the current diabetic pump and produce a live feed of blood glucose levels straight to a smartphone or watch.

General Information


Offers invasive free monitorization of blood glucose levels that can be viewed through our application to show real time feeds.
